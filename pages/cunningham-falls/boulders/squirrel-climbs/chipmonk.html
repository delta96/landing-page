Chip Monk

Grade: V2
Rating: 2.5/5
FA: Unkown

Information

Description

Chip Monk starts at the very end of the sloping arete, but only climbs using the holds on the face (the arete is off). Work your way left and using holds on the face. Top out just right of the adjacent face, same as Nut Dragger.

Directions

Chip Monk is located on the Squirrel Boulder on main wall. It starts at the base of the boulder just left of a yellow blaze.
