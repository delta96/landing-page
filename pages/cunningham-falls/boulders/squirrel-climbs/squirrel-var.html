Squirrel Variation

Grade: V2
Rating: 2.5/5
FA: Ezra Park

Information

Description

Squirrel Variation sit starts same as Ground Squirrel Don't Care, but only uses the holds on the face. Move up to couple crimp jugs, get a high heel hook, and reach high to a crimpy side pull. Once established, reposition your feet and go for the second jug rail and top out.

Directions

Squirrel Variation is located on the Squirrel Boulder on main wall, same as Ground Squirrel Don't Care. Obvious climb on the slight overhanging wall, left of Nut Dragger and Chip Monk.
